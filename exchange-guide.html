Understanding Exchange Rates: A Beginner’s Guide

Exchange rates determine how much one currency is worth when converted into another. These rates change constantly based on global market conditions.

1. What Is an Exchange Rate?

An exchange rate is the value of one currency compared to another. For example, 1 USD might be equal to 83 INR.

2. Why Do Rates Change?

Rates change due to various factors like inflation, interest rates, economic stability, and global demand for a currency.

3. Where Are Rates Used?

Exchange rates are essential for international trade, travel, investments, and remittances.
